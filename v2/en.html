The schedule

Visual schedules that turn everyday routines into a predictable flow

Create structured daily plans with custom imagery and icons so each next step feels clear, familiar, and easier to follow. Every activity is presented in order with calm visual guidance.

Flexible enough for mornings, school transitions, and evening routines — without locking the order into a rigid system.

Breakdowns

Break larger routines into calm, achievable steps

Complex activities become smaller actions children can understand, complete, and check off independently. Getting dressed becomes — Socks. Pants. Shirt.

Socks Pants Shirt

Progress

Track routines and notice where extra support helps most

See momentum over time and make better decisions around reminders, pacing, and encouragement. No grades, no scores — just patterns you can read and use when you need them.

Rewards

Small wins, made tangible

Celebrate completed routines with stars, custom goals, and meaningful rewards that build positive momentum — without turning daily life into a competition.

Privacy

Built for you — and no one else

No cloud sync by default. What you create stays on your device unless you choose otherwise. No ads, no data collection, no cross-app tracking.

On-device only Family routines stay where they belong.
No data collection No accounts required to use the app.
Works offline No network, subscription, or signup needed.

Cloud sync is optional, for those who want to share a profile across devices or with another parent. You turn it on when — and if — you need it.

About

Built out of necessity.
Designed with love.

As parents and professionals working with children with complex support needs, we couldn't find a visual scheduling tool that suited us. That is how Vigdís was born. No complicated sign-ups, no complex menus.

Just pure, local visual predictability to make daily life easier for families like ours.

Vigdís is named after the world's first democratically elected female president. She was also a teacher.
