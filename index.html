SIMPLIFY DAILY LIFE

Visual structure for daily routines

A user-friendly and local visual schedule app designed by professionals and parents to support individuals and families in getting a clear overview of activities of daily living.

Vigdís is a tool for anyone who wants increased predictability. Vigdís is designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals and is useful for those who use a wide range of services as well as everyone else.

🔒

Secure and local. Your family's data never leaves the device. No cloud, no tracking, no ads.

Built out of necessity.
Designed with love.

As parents and professionals working with children with complex support needs, we couldn't find a visual scheduling tool that suited us. That is how Vigdís was born. No complicated sign-ups, no complex menus.

Just pure, local visual predictability to make daily life easier for families like ours.
