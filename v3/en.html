01

How it works

Six modules. Each with a picture.

Each task is a square. Each square has a picture. The pictures are yours — from your camera roll, from the app's icon set, or drawn by the child themselves. The squares stack into morning, afternoon, and evening. The day becomes a pattern your family already knows.

Module 01 · Tasks

The day

The day, made of pictures.

Tasks appear in the right order, in the time block they belong to. The child sees what's next without asking. The parent sees what's done without checking.

Module 02 · Subtasks

Step by step

Big tasks become small ones.

"Get dressed" becomes — Socks. Pants. Shirt. Each step ticked off as it's done. Enough scaffolding to finish on their own.

Socks Pants Shirt

Module 03 · The week

Overview

The week in one frame.

Seven days on a single screen. See what's coming, what's done, and where tasks repeat — without flipping back and forth.

Module 04 · Rewards

Motivation

Stars that earn something.

Points earned across daily routines can become a movie night, an ice-cream trip, extra screen time. You decide what's on the list and what each one costs.

Module 05 · Patterns

Insight

See the pattern — not a grade.

A weekly overview shows where routines are working and where they're falling apart. No grades, no warnings — just enough to know where to put more support next week.

Module 06 · Sharing

Together

Same routine — two homes.

Scan a QR code to share a profile with a grandparent, a partner, or the other parent. Same tasks, same pictures, same stars — across two devices.

02

Who built Vigdís

A special-ed professional with ten years.

A small workshop in Reykjavík

Built by
someone who needed it first.

Vigdís started as a personal tool. A special-education professional and parent with over ten years of experience designing visual schedules for children with complex support needs went looking for an app that fit — and didn't find one that was both professional and simple to use.

That's how Vigdís came to be. No complicated sign-ups, no menus to figure out, nothing competing for the children's attention. We use it on our own kids before it gets into anyone else's hands.

Vigdís is named after Vigdís Finnbogadóttir — the world's first democratically elected female president. She was also a teacher before politics.

03

Privacy

On the device — not in the cloud.

Default

No signup.
No data collection.

Vigdís works locally. What you create lives on your iPhone or iPad — not on a server somewhere. No ads, no cross-app tracking, no accounts required to get started.

i.

On-device only

Family routines stay where they belong — on the device, not on a server.

ii.

No data collection

No accounts required, no cross-app tracking, no advertising.

iii.

Works offline

No network connection or signup needed to get started.

Optional

Cloud sync is optional — for parents who want to share a profile across devices or with another caregiver. Turn it on when you need it, off when you don't.
