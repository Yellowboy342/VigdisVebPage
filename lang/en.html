Built out of necessity.
Designed with love.

As parents and professionals working with children with complex support needs, we couldn't find a visual scheduling tool that suited us. That is how Vigdís was born. No complicated sign-ups, no complex menus.

Just pure, local visual predictability to make daily life easier for families like ours.

Meet Lóa

The bird who tells you a new rhythm has begun.

Lóa is a golden plover — the bird whose return to Iceland traditionally announces the start of spring. In Vigdís she’s the quiet companion who whispers “next step is here” and celebrates with you when the day is done. She never rushes. She never guilts.

She turns up in the small moments: when a task is checked off, when the day is winding down, when you need a tiny nudge to keep going.

“No deadlines. No blame. Just the next step.” — Lóa
